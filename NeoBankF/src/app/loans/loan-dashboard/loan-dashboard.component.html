How to Apply for a Loan

1
Choose a Loan Product
Browse available loan types — Personal, Home, Auto, Education and more. Each product has its own rate, tenure and amount range.
2
Set Amount & Tenure
Use the EMI calculator to pick a comfortable amount and repayment period. Your monthly EMI is shown live before you submit.
3
Review & Submit
Confirm your details on the review screen and submit. Your application status will be visible here and on the My Loans page.
4
Approval & Repayment
Once approved by admin, funds are disbursed. Pay your monthly EMIs from the Repayment Schedule page to keep your account in good standing.
